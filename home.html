Hello and Welcome to Musical Mood Generator

Please use the options at the bottom of the page to choose your current mood from the dropdown menu. Your given mood will then randomly generate a personalized playlist from Spotify that corresponds to your given mood. You will also find the playlist comes with a handy-dandy GIF that similiarly corresponds to your mood. You can save up to 8 moods, happy generating!

Instructions

Selet a mood option from the dropdown menu on your right!
